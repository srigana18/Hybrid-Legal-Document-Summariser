An Act relating to public health emergency preparedness.

Section 1. The Department of Health shall establish a statewide emergency response framework to coordinate with hospitals, local health agencies, and emergency management offices.

Section 2. The framework shall include:
1. Medical supply tracking and distribution procedures,
2. Real-time data reporting requirements,
3. Minimum staffing levels during declared public health emergencies.

Section 3. The Department shall submit an annual report to the Governor and Legislature summarizing statewide readiness and recommending improvements.
